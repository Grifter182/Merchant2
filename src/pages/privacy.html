Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, use and protect your information.

Last updated: August 5, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Business information: business name, address, industry type, tax ID
  • Owner details: name, contact details, identity document information
  • Payment information: bank account details, cardholder data
  • Transaction data: amount, date, merchant name/location, method
  • Technical data: IP address, device/browser type, cookies
  • Biometric and identity verification data for enhanced security or fraud prevention

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and manage payment services
  • Verify identities and prevent fraud
  • Improve our products and services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Communicate with merchants and customers

3. Sharing of Information

  • Our partners and affiliates
  • Financial institutions and card networks
  • Fraud prevention services
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Service providers assisting in payment operations

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to track user behaviour and improve your experience. You can manage your preferences via your browser settings or using our cookie consent banner.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal and regulatory obligations. When data is no longer required, we securely dispose of or anonymise it.

6. Security Measures

  • PCI DSS compliance with industry‑standard protections
  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls limited to essential personnel
  • Continuous monitoring, audits and security assessments
  • Regular employee training on data protection

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletion
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a regulator
