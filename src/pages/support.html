Support

Dedicated, human support backed by comprehensive documentation and automation tools.

Knowledge base & live chat

Find answers fast with our self‑serve knowledge base. Browse step‑by‑step guides, API references and best practices. Need a hand? Start a live chat with one of our experienced support agents directly from your Merchant Portal for real‑time assistance.

Developer Slack & email

Connect with our engineering team and other developers in our dedicated Slack community. Get tips on integration, share feedback and collaborate on new features. Prefer email? Reach us any time at support@merchanthaus.io and we’ll respond promptly.
