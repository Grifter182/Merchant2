Compliance & Security

Trust is earned, not assumed. We safeguard every transaction from end to end.

PCI DSS Level 1 & tokenization

Protecting cardholder data is our top priority. MerchantHaus.io is certified to the highest PCI DSS Level 1 standard and our infrastructure is audited regularly to maintain compliance. Tokenized payment credentials mean sensitive card data never touches your servers, dramatically reducing your PCI scope and exposure.

Advanced fraud & 3‑D Secure

Prevent chargebacks before they happen with our layered fraud defenses. AI‑driven risk scoring uses machine learning and 30 billion transactions to detect anomalies while 3‑D Secure 2 adds an additional layer of authentication for online payments. Customisable velocity and geolocation rules empower you to fine tune risk tolerance.

Secure webhook & hosting

All webhooks are signed using HMAC‑SHA256 so you can verify payload integrity. Our services run in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centres with end‑to‑end encryption. Whether you’re ingesting webhooks or hosting your own payment forms, you can trust that your data stays private and protected.

Security without compromise

Stay compliant and protect your customers. Partner with MerchantHaus.io to get the latest in payments security and peace of mind.
